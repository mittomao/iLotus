Full Day
 08.30 - 09:15: Pick up your hotel or meeting point (around the Old Quarter) and start the trip with 2.5 hours to the harbor by high way route with 01 short break on half way for about 20 minutes.
 12:00 - 12:15: Arrive Tuan Chau harbor, check on the boat and enjoy Vietnamese set menu lunch while cruising through the amazing rocks: Incense burner, cock fighting...
13:30 - 14:00 : visit the biggest and the most beautiful cave of Ha Long bay – Amazing cave
14:45 - 15:15 : Discover Luon cave (water cave) with some options:
  • Kayaking or bamboo rowing boat.
  • Relaxing on the big boat if you prefer to have more free time by yourself
15:45- 16:00 : Visit Titov Island.
  • Swimming on the beach
  • Trekking up to Titov peak for taking photos with panoramic view of Ha Long bay.
17:00 - 17:30 : Tea break time on sundeck to enjoy sunset
17:30 - 17:45 : Back to Ha Long harbor.
20:00 - 20:30: Arrival to Ha Noi Drop you off at your hotel (around old quarter). End of trip.
