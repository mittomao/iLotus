Welcome to LEMC

Welcome to LEDMC! LEDMC is a team of people with a full of passion, energy and experience. LEDMC was set up by principal aim to bring satisfaction and pleasure to our clients by giving them our best service with best price. We offer private tours and group joining tours in Asia. Tours includes selective day trips, hotels, cruises, transfers and hospitable guides to the best sightseeing spots. We can customize the package tours to suit our clients budget. We want to show you Asia’s wonderful natural beauty. The Asia region is our life; we live and breathe the culture, traditions and beliefs every moment of every day. We’re very proud of the greatness of our homeland and its neighbors, and it’s our absolute pleasure to show you everything we love about it.
LEDMC helps you to save your valuable time and provide you package tours with high quality. We have a devoted team of tour guides who can speak English, French, Spanish, Russian, Japanese, Chinese and other languages. Their professionalism, personality and outstanding knowledge are the reasons they are employed by us. We will try our best to make your holidays memorable.
